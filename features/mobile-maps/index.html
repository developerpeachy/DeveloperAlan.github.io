Mobile and Tablet Maps with on map directions

Make maps for your mobile and tablet devices with on map directions. Use the normal steps to make a map and your maps will automatically render on the popular mobile devices and tablets.

The devices currently supported are:

  1. iPhones, iTouch, and iPad
  2. Android Phones and Tablets
  3. Blackberry Phones
  4. Windows Phones

Mobile devices include on map directions, and the ability to select multiple categories on the maps. Due to the screen size mobile devices do not include filters. IPads include filters, search capability, on map directions, and the ability to select multiple categories. Android tablets function similar to phones due to lacking support for certain features.

Get ready to make your own map of your data on a variety of devices!
