Create KML for Google Earth

Once you map your addresses, and save your map with an email address. An email will be sent containing a url to download the newly created KML file. Upon modifying the map, the KML will be updated along with the map.

KML is an XML formatted language used by Google Earth, Google Maps, and other programs to allow geo-spatial data to be rendered.

To create a KML file from your data, do the following:

  1. Go to EasyMapMaker.com
  2. Copy and paste data from your spreadsheet into our form
  3. Set the appropriate options (optional)
  4. Click "Make Map"
  5. Click "Launch Map Save"
  6. Be sure to check "Generate KML for use with Google Earth."
  7. Fill in your email address and save the map
  8. Open your email and find the link to the kml file
