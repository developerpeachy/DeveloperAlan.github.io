Maps are an integral part of life, see how easy mapping can be

Most of us use maps everyday. EasyMapMaker was designed to make custom google maps easily from your excel or spreadsheet data. It accepts the same location information that you would expect from google, including addresses, zip codes, cities, states, and latitude and longitude coordinates.

It's really this easy

  1. Copy and paste data from your spreadsheet into our form
  2. Set the appropriate options (optional)
  3. (Optional for advanced maps) Upload your custom logo (45x45 ideally) and/or upload your own markers/pins
  4. (Optional for advanced maps) Set the items to display and filters
  5. Click "Make Map" (all done!)

Check out the sample video for how to make a basic map.

Overview of making a map with easymapmaker.com Mapping is this easy. Make a basic map with http://www.EasyMapMaker.com with a few clicks. If you have a data with location information in a spreadsheet, you can quickly make a google map.
